About Ninya
"The world would be a better place if everybody did what they love instead of being stuck."

Ninya started as a conversation. Not a strategy session — just thinking out loud with an AI that could finally keep up with the way my brain moves. One thread led to another. A gut health rabbit hole. A business framework. A long honest look at forty years of feeling like I was always in the wrong room.

Somewhere in all of it I realized the thing I'd been trying to build my whole life was just this: a place to be honest about the journey. Ninya is that place.

Ryan Ninness

Founder, Ninya
